My humble opinion of the 3 best movies of all-time


1.Star wars chapter 3

"Star Wars: Episode III - Revenge of the Sith" is often regarded as the best in the saga for its gripping narrative, emotional depth, and stunning visuals. It masterfully depicts Anakin Skywalker's tragic fall to the dark side, delivering unforgettable moments like his betrayal of the Jedi and the heartbreaking demise of Padmé Amidala. The film's epic lightsaber duels and exploration of political intrigue add to its appeal, making it a cinematic masterpiece that continues to resonate with audiences.

2.Both movies of Harry Potter chapter 7

"Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 1 and Part 2" are celebrated as the apex of the series. Part 1 sets a dark and intense tone as Harry, Ron, and Hermione embark on their mission. Part 2 delivers a thrilling climax with the epic battle at Hogwarts. Both films showcase stellar performances, stunning visuals, and satisfying character resolutions, making them the ultimate culmination of the beloved Harry Potter saga.

3.How to train your dragon 2

"How to Train Your Dragon 2" excels as the best in the franchise due to its expanded world-building, deep character development, and thrilling action. It introduces new dragon species and lands, deepens Hiccup and Toothless' bond, and introduces a compelling villain, all while delivering breathtaking animation and exhilarating sequences.
